Have Any Questions?

Answers to common questions about JOTONE's services, materials, and process.

Ask on WhatsApp

Bitumen is a tar-based material that becomes brittle in heat and cracks under Bangladesh's temperature swings. Liquid membranes (PU and Acrylic) remain flexible, bond seamlessly to surfaces without joints, and maintain their protective properties for over a decade.

Our materials come with a 10-year warranty backed by the manufacturer. With proper care and our recommended annual inspection, many applications perform well beyond 15 years.

We design our work schedule around your household. Most rooftop applications are completed within 1–2 days and interior treatments within a day, with minimal fumes and no heavy machinery inside your home.

We work on all property types — apartments, independent homes, commercial buildings, and multi-storey residential complexes. For apartments, we coordinate directly with building management.

Any performance issue covered under warranty is repaired at no cost to you. We also offer an annual maintenance programme to catch potential issues before they become problems.
